
The Tomb of Praecor Loth
Here are the maps of the Tomb of Praecor Loth, a mighty king whose realm fell to the Guardian long ago.

Tomb of Praecor Loth, Level 1
To get the Sword of Stonestrike, you can open the portcullis by placing a weight on the southern pillar in the room to the west. Alternately magic allows the portcullis to be unlocked.
A rock hammer allows you to get to the gem and treasure in the NW passage.Use the Scepter there.
There are portions of maps of the third level down all passages except the NW.

Tomb of Praecor Loth, Level 2
Helena will reward a brave individual.

Tomb of Praecor Loth, Level 3
The Vest of Flameproof is very good to have.
The marked key is an essential item if you chose not to destroy the guardians on the fourth level.

Tomb of Praecor Loth, Level 4
Morpheus uses poison.
To open Umbria's gateway move one of the candles on the far side of his room to the east.
Lethe is the toughest opponent you will meet.
Don't be afraid to challenge Praecor Loth with the truth.
