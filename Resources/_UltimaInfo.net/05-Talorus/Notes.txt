
Talorus
Here are the maps of Talorus, alien world of mysterious beings.

Talorus, Level 1
Talorus, Level 1
The Crystal manifests in the ceiling of the central chamber. There are bounce pads in the room which let you get up to it. The faster you move the higher you bounce.
The Bliy Skup chamber is where the Scepter needs to be used.
There are Kal and Corp runes in the Runekeeper's stocks.

Talorus, Level 2
Talorus, Level 2
Be prepared for a fight down here.
You get teleported to the Crystal in the center of the lava by the Historian when you do him a favour.
There is a trigger to create a bridge back to the Skup chamber there.
